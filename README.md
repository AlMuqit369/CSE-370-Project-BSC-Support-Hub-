Project Title: BSC Support Hub – A Web Platform for UndergraduateAcademic & Career Growth

Idea Summary:

A platform designed specifically for BSC students to get centralized support in
academics, career planning, networking, and mental well-being. The goal is to
create a student-first environment with categorized help: resources, mentorship,
opportunities, and peer collaboration.

Required Feature List (excluding Login/Signup):

1. Smart Resource Navigator
a. Filter and search academic materials by subject, course, university, or
topic.
b. Supports uploading, downloading, and preview of files (PDFs, slides,
videos).

3. Mentor Connect
a. Request mentorship via form.
b. Schedule meeting slots (Google Meet/Zoom integration optional).

5. Job and Internship Tracker
a. Aggregates latest job/internship postings for BSC-level students.
b. Filter by location, field, experience level.

7. Announcement Board
a. Platform-wide notices about academic competitions, scholarships,
conferences, or student programs.
b. Admin/teacher-only posting.

9. Personalized Dashboard
a. Shows saved jobs, joined mentor chats, bookmarked resources, and
event reminders.
b. Academic progress tracker or weekly goal checklist (basic UI).

11. Notifications and Alerts
a. Email confirmation on registration
b. Notifications for new events from subscribed clubs
c. Event reminder
